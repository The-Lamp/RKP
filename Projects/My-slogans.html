My Own Slogans

Yeh wo alfaaz hain jo sirf shabdon tak simit nahi, balki ek mindset ko reflect karte hain. Ye slogans un logo ke liye hain jo duniya ke rulebook se nahi, apni terms pe jeena seekhte hain. Har line ek nayi soch ko jagati hai, ek aisi journey shuru karti hai jo sirf daredevils ke liye hoti hai. Jab duniya khamosh hoti hai, tab asli game wahi se shuru hota hai. Ye mere apne thoughts hain – thoda dark, thoda unpredictable – bilkul meri tarah.

"har shortcut ek badlaav ka signal hota hai."
"aapka data ek jackpot hai, aur unki strategy aapki life ka last game."
"समय आ गया है चुप्पी तोड़ने का, सारे दिमागी मरीज पागल नहीं होते"
"इंटरनेट के किसी कोने में,आओ गुमनाम हो जाए कही!!"
"Control unhi ke haath hota hai, jo system ke rules likhte hain, follow karne wale hamesha gulam hi rahenge."
"Andhera sirf darr nahi, ek taqat hai... jo dikhai nahi deta, wahi sabse khatarnaak hota hai."
"Risk lene ka maza tab hi aata hai jab har move me kuch khona ho."
"Duniya ko badalne ka sapna mat dekh, apni duniya ka king ban."
